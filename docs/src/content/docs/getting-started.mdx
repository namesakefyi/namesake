Hello! This guide is for people who are interested in contributing to [Namesake](https://namesake.fyi). We're glad you are here.

## Background

Legally changing names can be time-consuming and expensive. It intersects dozens of different institutions, from state and local courts to federal agencies. Documentation is scattered, full of legalese, and rarely speaks directly to trans experiences.

Namesake exists to fill the gaps.

1. We write step-by-step name change [guides](https://namesake.fyi/guides) using plain language, surfacing communal insights, and answering frequently asked questions.
2. We create interactive, accessible [web forms](https://namesake.fyi/forms) to help fill out legal documents.
3. We publish [writing](https://namesake.fyi/blog) and other media.
4. We provide [community support](https://namesake.fyi/chat) and encouragement.

All of our tooling is free and open source, and always will be.

We are volunteer-driven, and we welcome contributions from a wide range of people—trans and cis, technical and non-technical.

## How to contribute

To help contribute, first join the [Namesake Discord server](https://namesake.fyi/chat). This is where chat and coordination takes place. Say hello in `#introductions`!

### Answer name change questions

Do you have experience with the name change process in your state? Check the `#help` channel on [Discord](https://namesake.fyi/chat) for threads where you can share your knowledge.

### Contribute writing

We welcome writing contributions in the form of name change guides, blog posts, or any other writing you'd like Namesake to publish. Reach out on [Discord](https://namesake.fyi/chat) or email [hey@namesake.fyi](mailto:hey@namesake.fyi).

### Write code

If you know some HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and want to help fix bugs or build new features for Namesake, start by getting your development environment set up.

You can also share your feedback and ideas in the `#feedback-ideas` channel on [Discord](https://namesake.fyi/chat).

### Donate money to support us

Namesake is a registered 501(c)(3) non-profit with fiscal sponsorship from [Superbloom](https://superbloom.design). Donations help us fund continued development, maintenance, outreach, and hosting.

We don't pay for any ads or marketing: all of our funds go directly toward the development of Namesake. As a volunteer-led team with a small budget, any donation is greatly appreciated and really goes a long way toward making this work possible.
